Crystal Collector

Click the Match Me button below to be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until uou click on it.

The value of the crystals will change each time the game starts.
